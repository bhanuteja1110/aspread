What is Spread?

Spread is an enterprise building practical local services: consumer apps for ordering, partner apps for vendors & riders, and admin tools for order management and analytics.

Product lines

  • Spread (consumer): Orders for food, groceries, bakery & essentials.
  • Partner App: Vendor & rider portal to accept and manage orders.
  • Order Management: Admin dashboard for operations and analytics.
  • Silent: Privacy-first messaging experience.
  • Gift Task Tracker: Web app for rewards & task management.

How it works — simple flow

  1. Discover: Browse categories and partnered vendors.
  2. Order: Add items, choose payment, and place order.
  3. Assign & Pickup: Partner accepts; rider picks up.
  4. Track: Real-time tracking (geo + ETA).
  5. Deliver & Rate: Delivery completes — rating & feedback.

Built on Firebase (auth, Firestore, storage) and designed to keep initial costs low while enabling growth.
