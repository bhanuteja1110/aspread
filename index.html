What is Spread?

Spread is an enterprise that builds simple, fast, and beautiful apps to connect people and local businesses. It includes consumer-facing apps (ordering, browsing), partner apps for vendors and delivery partners, and internal tools like order management and task trackers.

Core product lines

  • Spread (consumer): App for placing orders (food, grocery, bakery, essentials).
  • Partner App: For restaurants, vendors and delivery partners to accept/manage orders.
  • Order Management: Admin dashboard to track orders, riders, payments and analytics.
  • Silent: Messaging / privacy-first app experience (example project).
  • Gift Task Tracker: Web app for managing rewards, tasks and gifts.

How it works — simple flow

  1. Discover: User opens Spread, browses categories and partnered vendors.
  2. Order: Add items to cart, choose payment or cash-on-delivery, and place order.
  3. Assign & Pickup: Partner accepts order; a rider picks up the order.
  4. Track: User tracks the order in real-time (geolocation + ETA).
  5. Deliver & Rate: Order delivered; user rates experience & merchant.

Each step is backed by small, focused services: authentication (Firebase), realtime DB (Firestore), storage, and notifications — built to scale while keeping costs low during initial growth.
